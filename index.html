Who are We?

The Madison Track association is a team out of Jackson, TN from Madison Academic Magnet High School and is made up of a well committed group of student runners. The team consists of about 30 plus runners. Coach Maxwell and the assistant coaches, Brian, Cam, and Carson, are responsible for making sure that all the runners get a great run in at every practice. The runners believe that more training is better running, which is what helps them strive for excellence. Below are some quotes that the team has created to motivate themselves, as well as the other teammates.

  • To be unstoppable, you must be unbreakable.
  • To be unbreakable, you must first break youself.
  • To break yourself, you must be willing to endure.
  • To endure, you must endure the pain.

Our Purpose

We believe that everyone has the opportunity to do something great. What we want from our atheletes is that they push themselves and never give up. We want every student on our team to feel that they have gave it their all after their run. We hope to instill greatness and perserverance into these kids. We never want any kid to think that they are not good enough to run.
