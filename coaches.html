Coach Maxwell

Coach maxwell has been a coach at Madison for many years now. He has continually shown support to all sports. This year he is taking on a very important role as being the department head of sports for Madison Academic. Coach Maxwell is very determined to help the track team in all aspects of training.

Coach Brian

Coach Brian, just like Coach Maxwell, believes that he can make a difference in how hard the team trains. He recently had a son graduate from Madison Academic, so he knows a little about how to work with the kids here. Coach Brian wants this track team to be the best there ever was and will be. So he trains the runners like there won't be a tomorrow.

Coach Carson

Coach Carson is married to a conselor for Madison Academic. He handles the sprinter side of the team. He trains them with the very best sprinting techniques and technology. Coach Carson is very aware of what is happening on the track. He is the medical side of the team.

Coach Cam

Coach Cam is a recent graduate of Madison Academic and trained under Coach Brian and Coach Maxwell. He understands how hard it is and encourages the runners to keep running. He handles the stretching and the sprinters, similar to Coach Carson.
